Energy Consumption Data (Raw)
Source: U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)
Link: https://www.eia.gov/opendata/

The file eia_energy_consumption_raw.csv was manually downloaded from the
U.S. Energy Information Administration Open Data portal.

Due to file size limitations, the raw energy consumption dataset is not
included in this repository. Reviewers can re-download the dataset
directly from the source link above.


Weather Data (Raw)
Source: NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI)
API Endpoint: https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/services/data/v1
Dataset: daily-summaries
Station ID: USW00093814 (Cincinnati CVG Airport)
Date Range: 2022-01-01 to 2023-12-31

The weather dataset was collected programmatically using the NOAA API.
The raw output was saved locally as weather_cincinnati_daily_raw.csv
before cleaning and aggregation.
